This is a guided list and descriptions of the various hiking trails in Bellingham, Washington.

Arroyo Park

Located off of Chuckanut Drive, this hike is an easy beginner level trail. Providing beautiful scenery of the Chuckanut mountain. When completing the full route it is roughly 8.3 miles to Lost Lake, with an elavtion gain of 1,686 ft.

Oyster Dome

Oyster Dome is also located off of Chukanut drive and is considered a fairly popular hike. This hike is considered on more of the challenging and difficult side due to its elvation gain of about 1,879 ft within a 7.3 mile hike. The top of the trail leads to a beautiful scene of many of the PNW islands scattered around the Pudget Sound.

Interurban Trail

The Interurban Trail is located between Fairhaven and Larrabee State Park, this trail links to many other trails and hikes along Fairhaven and Chuckanut. This trail would be considered an easy level due to its lack of elevation gain and fairly flat trail.
