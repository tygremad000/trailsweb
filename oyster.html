Oyster Dome

Oyster Dome is a very popular but challenging hike for beginners. With steep switch backs and decent elevation gain it can be challenging but, the view on top of this trail is unreal. When I had first gone on this trail I had heard alot about its viewpoint but, experiencing it in person was unbelievable. The view of the SanJuan islands and of the Pudget Sound is well worth the challenging hike! This a great hike in the summer and late summer months on clear days for better visibilty of the top view.
