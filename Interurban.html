Interurban Trail

This trail is particularly fun because it is so well connected with the other trails on Chukanut and in Fairhaven. One of my favorite off trails on the Interurban trail is a loaction called teddy bear cove. Right on the Pudget Sound this was one of the first beaches I went to when I came to Bellingham and it is absolutely worth a visit if youre planning on hiking the Interurban trail!
